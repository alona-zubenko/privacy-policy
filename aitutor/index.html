AITutor — Privacy Policy

Effective: 28 April 2026 · Contact: platmargo@gmail.com

1. Summary

AITutor lets a student photograph a school or university question and receive an AI-generated explanation. The app offers three usage modes:

  • Free tier — 1 question/day routed through our backend (Vercel · Frankfurt, EU + Supabase EU) so we can fairly allocate the daily allowance per device.
  • Paid packs — additional questions purchased via Apple App Store / Google Play in-app billing. Balance is maintained on our backend.
  • Advanced (BYO) mode — you provide your own AI provider access keys; AITutor calls the provider directly from your device. Our backend is not contacted for these requests.

3. Data we DO NOT collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number — there is no account to create. Identification is by random device UUID.
  • Your precise or approximate location.
  • Audio, contacts, calendar.
  • Health, financial, or biometric data.
  • Web browsing history.

4. Sub-processors

When you use the Free or Paid tier, the photo and the question text are sent to one of:

  • Anthropic PBC (USA) — privacy policy. Vision + reasoning model.
  • Groq Inc. (USA) — privacy policy. Faster vision + reasoning chain.

Receipts are verified with:

  • Apple Inc. (USA) via App Store Server API.
  • Google LLC (USA) via Google Play Developer API.

Our backend itself runs on:

  • Vercel Inc. (USA, EU region fra1) — serverless functions.
  • Supabase Inc. (EU region) — Postgres database, AES-256 at rest.

In Advanced (BYO) mode, requests are sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider. Our backend is not involved and we have no record of those requests.

5. Camera and photo permissions

We request camera and photo library access strictly to capture an image of your homework question. The image is resized to 1024 px JPEG before any transmission. In Free / Paid mode it is sent to our backend → forwarded to the AI provider → discarded once the response is returned. In Advanced (BYO) mode it is sent directly from your device to your AI provider. We never train models on your photos.

6. AI-generated content

Answers are produced by third-party large language models (Anthropic Claude, Groq Llama, or your own provider in BYO mode). They may occasionally be inaccurate, biased, or off-topic. Please verify critical information independently. Each answer in the app has a Report action — tap the flag icon to send the answer + your reason to our moderation queue.

7. In-app purchases

Consumable packs are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see your payment method or credit card. After purchase, we receive only the transaction ID, product ID, environment (Sandbox / Production), and raw signed receipt — used to verify the transaction is genuine. Refund and cancellation requests must be made through Apple Support or Google Play; if the platform sends us a refund notification, we automatically reduce your credit balance accordingly.

8. Account deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • In-app: Settings → "Delete account" → confirm twice. Removes your users, credits, content_reports rows immediately; anonymises purchases rows (sets user_id = NULL but retains the row for accounting). The app then resets to onboarding with a fresh random device UUID.
  • Web: follow the instructions at delete-account.html to email platmargo@gmail.com with a deletion request.

Deletion is irreversible. Anonymised purchase records may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax and accounting law.

9. Security

  • All data in transit uses HTTPS / TLS 1.2+.
  • Supabase Postgres encrypts data at rest (AES-256).
  • BYO API keys are stored on your device using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore (via expo-secure-store) — encrypted, not extractable by other apps, not included in iCloud Backup or Android allowBackup bundle.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs.

10. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Even though we hold very little personal data (a random UUID, balance, optional reports), you have the right to:

  • Access — email us your device UUID and we will return all data we hold for it within 30 days.
  • Erasure — use the in-app "Delete account" flow, or email us with your device UUID. Anonymised purchase records may be retained as noted above.
  • Object — to processing for fraud prevention; this means we cannot continue to provide the Free / Paid tiers, but Advanced (BYO) mode remains available.
  • Lodge a complaint — with your local Data Protection Authority. For Ukraine: Ombudsman. For EU: your national authority.

Email: platmargo@gmail.com

11. Children's privacy

AITutor is rated 12+ / Teen. Under-13 children should use the app under parental supervision in jurisdictions where COPPA-equivalent rules apply. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.
