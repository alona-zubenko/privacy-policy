Delete your AITutor account and data

AITutor is account-less by design — there is no email, password, or social login. Your data is identified by a random UUID generated on your device the first time you launch the app (aitutor:deviceId:v1).

Option 1: Delete in-app (recommended)

  1. Open the AITutor app.
  2. Go to Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm twice (Apple/Google review require a two-step confirmation).
  5. The app immediately calls our delete-account endpoint, wipes local data, generates a fresh device UUID, and returns to onboarding.
What gets deleted: your device UUID, paid credit balance, free-tier usage flag, content reports.
What is retained anonymously: purchase rows in our accounting log (Apple/Google transaction IDs only, no link to you). Retained for up to 7 years to comply with tax and accounting law.
What never existed: we never collected your name, email, location, contacts, photos library, or browsing history. There is nothing else to delete.

Option 2: Email request

If you cannot access the app (for example, you uninstalled before deleting):

  1. Email platmargo@gmail.com with subject "AITutor account deletion request".
  2. If you still know your device UUID, include it. Otherwise describe the approximate purchase date — we can find recent transactions by your Apple/Google email.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 7 days and complete the deletion within 30 days, in compliance with GDPR Article 17.

Refunds for unused credits

Apple App Store and Google Play handle refund requests directly. To request a refund:

  • iOS: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Android: Play Store → Order history → Request a refund

If the platform issues a refund, our backend automatically reduces your credit balance to reflect the refund.

Questions

Email platmargo@gmail.com. We typically respond within 2–3 business days.
