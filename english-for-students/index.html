Privacy Policy

App: English for Students
Developer: Alona Zubenko
Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Data We Collect

English for Students does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information (vocabulary progress, test results, word bank) is stored locally on your device only using the device's built-in storage.

2. Internet Access

The app uses an internet connection only to download over-the-air updates (new content or bug fixes) via Expo Updates. No personal data is sent during this process.

3. In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional one-time In-App Purchase ($4.99) to unlock the full course. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive, store, or process any payment or billing information.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses iOS Text-to-Speech (built into the operating system) to pronounce English words. This feature is processed entirely on your device and does not send data to any server.

5. Analytics & Advertising

The app contains no analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at https://alona-zubenko.github.io/privacy-policy/english-for-students/ with a new date.

8. Contact

Questions? Contact us at: alona.zubenko@icloud.com
